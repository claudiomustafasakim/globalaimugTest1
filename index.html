    About Us

    GLOBAL AIM is Non-governmental Organization NGO registered at district and national level with NGO Board with Reg. No (S.5914/7350).

    The Organization is operational in the three Districts of Adjumani, Obongi and Moyo Districts in west Nile Region in Northern part of Uganda

    RATIONALE.

    GLOBAL AIM was established to run sustainable development programs to improve livelihood of the community and is supported by strong policies, adequate trainings and logistical services that facilitate the provision of these services.

    • GLOBAL AIM’S sustainable development program is envisaged to create an empowered community with improved livelihoods.
    • GLOBAL AIM’S sustainable development program is envisaged to create an empowered community with improved livelihoods..
    • GLOBAL AIM in her community intervention targets the youth, women, Children, Orphans and other Vulnerable children, people living with HIV/AIDS, Refugees, Internal displaced persons (IDPS), armed forces, people with special needs (PSN) and community groups as the primary program beneficiaries while the duty bearers and the civil society organizations forms part of the secondary beneficiaries.

    The program to a greater extent improves information flow and technical skills of community for improved livelihoods. This will in turn enhance the capacity of the community to prevent and mitigate the impact of Diseases, increased household income for self-reliance and, conducive environment for peaceful co-existence and quality service delivery.
